## About

I'm a PhD candidate at UM6P specializing in **deep learning for breast cancer screening** — working at the intersection of computer vision, medical imaging, and clinical AI. My research focuses on designing hybrid architectures that are not only accurate, but interpretable enough to support real clinical decisions.

My work spans CNNs, Vision Transformers, ensemble methods, self-supervised learning, and Explainable AI (XAI). I'm particularly interested in multimodal fusion — combining mammography and ultrasound to improve screening outcomes.
